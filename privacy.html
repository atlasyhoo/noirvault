Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2026
Core Statement: NoirVault does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or financial data. All operations are performed locally on your device.

Welcome to NoirVault (the "App"). We believe privacy is a fundamental right — especially when it comes to your personal wealth. This App is designed with a "local-first" architecture, giving you a completely private and secure space to manage your asset portfolio.

1. Data Collection Declaration

We do not collect any data.

  • No User Accounts: The App does not require registration, login, or any personal information such as email, phone number, or name.
  • No Data Upload: All asset records, portfolio data, PIN settings, and preferences are stored strictly on your local device and are never sent to our servers or any third party.
  • No Device Identifiers: We do not collect IDFA, IDFV, IP addresses, or device serial numbers.
  • No Third-Party Analytics: This App does not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs (e.g., Firebase, Google Analytics, AppsFlyer).

2. How Data is Stored

All records — including asset holdings, portfolio values, acquisition history, private notes, and app settings — are stored in your device's local storage (on-device SQLite database and iOS Sandbox).

  • If you delete the App, all data will be permanently lost as we do not maintain cloud backups.
  • If you uninstall and reinstall the App, previous records cannot be recovered unless you have exported a backup file.
  • You can export a full backup of your data at any time via the Settings page, and restore it after reinstallation.
  • You can manually delete individual assets or clear all data at any time within the App settings.

3. Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, there is no sharing, selling, or renting of data to any third parties.

4. Financial Disclaimer

NoirVault is a personal asset tracking and organization tool. The information displayed within the App — including asset values, portfolio totals, and distribution charts — is based solely on data you manually enter. This App does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, tax guidance, or legal counsel. Always consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.

5. Children's Privacy

Since this App does not collect any personal information, it does not knowingly collect data from children. The App is intended for adult users managing personal financial assets.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we decide to change our data practices in the future (though we have no current plans to do so), we will update this policy and notify you prominently. For the current version and the foreseeable future, NoirVault will maintain its "Zero Data Collection" principle.
