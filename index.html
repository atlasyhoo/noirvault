Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Does the app work offline?

Yes. NoirVault is fully offline. All features — asset registration, portfolio dashboard, structure charts, wealth timeline, and settings — work without any internet connection. Your data never leaves your device.

Is my financial data private?

Absolutely. All asset data is stored locally on your device using on-device SQLite storage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your information on external servers. No accounts or cloud sync are required.

What happens if I forget my PIN?

For security reasons, there is no PIN recovery mechanism. If you forget your PIN, you will need to reinstall the app. Please note that reinstalling will permanently delete all locally stored asset data.

What are Hidden Holdings?

Hidden Holdings are assets protected by a secondary PIN. They are invisible in the main dashboard until you authenticate with your secondary PIN. This allows you to keep sensitive assets discreet.

What should I do if the app crashes or freezes?

Try restarting the app first. If the issue persists, clear the app cache or reinstall it. If problems continue, please contact us at atlas19870918@gmail.com with your device model and a description of the issue.

Which currencies are supported?

NoirVault supports AED, USD, EUR, GBP, SAR, QAR, KWD, BHD, OMR, CHF, SGD, and CNY. You can set your preferred display currency in Settings.

About the App

NoirVault is a private wealth management app designed for high-net-worth individuals who demand discretion, clarity, and full control over their asset portfolio. Track real estate, energy investments, sovereign funds, equities, gold, luxury assets, cash deposits, and more — all within a secure, offline-first vault on your device.

Important Notice: NoirVault is a personal asset tracking tool for organizational purposes only. It does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, or legal guidance. Always consult a qualified financial advisor for investment decisions.

How It Works

  1. Set Your PIN: On first launch, set a PIN to protect your vault. Optionally set a secondary PIN for hidden holdings.
  2. Register Your Assets: Add your holdings — real estate, gold, equities, cash, luxury assets, and more — with full details.
  3. View Your Dashboard: See your total portfolio value, category breakdown, and recent holdings at a glance.
  4. Analyze Structure: Use the Portfolio Structure page to visualize distribution by category, liquidity, or status.
  5. Track History: Review your Wealth Timeline to see how your portfolio has grown over the years.
